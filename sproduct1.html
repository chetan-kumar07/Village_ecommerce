Dasheri Mango

₹300 per 5 kg

Product Details

Dasehri Mango is a renowned variety of mango originating from the Malihabad region in Uttar Pradesh, India. It is cherished for its exquisite taste and aroma. Dasehri mangoes are medium-sized, with a pale yellow skin and a slightly fibrous yet juicy flesh. They are known for their sweet and tangy flavor, making them a delightful treat for mango lovers. With their unique blend of sweetness and acidity, Dasehri mangoes have earned a special place in the hearts of mango connoisseurs, both in India and around the world.
